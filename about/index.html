About Me

My name is Ryan and I'm currently an undergraduate student at San Diego State University studying computer science. I like programming, tech, astrophotography, and K-pop (stan Aespa).

This Site

This was originally supposed to be a place to showcase projects and other stuff I'm working on that were relevant to comp-sci/programming, aka career stuff.

However, I've thought about creating content around my interests which expands into tech and astronomy.

I could make videos (any maybe I will) but they require a lot of work. So for now, I thought I'd just write articles about the things I've wanted to share and talk about. It's simple enough and I like writing, especially if I know it'll help someone out, whatever way that might be.

On some technical notes, I've tried my best to minimize the footprint of the site in terms of resources and rendering so everything is quite minimal. It's just HTML, CSS, and a single JS script for content reusability.

I have an article discussing the reasons why I've created the site in this way so I won't go into it here but I suggest checking it out.
